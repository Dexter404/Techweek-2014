About Techweek

"An investment in knowledge pays the best interest."

-Benjamin Franklin.

With this belief, the academic session resumes with TECHWEEK every year. TECHWEEK is the annual workshop galore, which offers budding engineers an insight into the various new and upcoming technologies. For many years, TECHWEEK has served as a platform for students to be introduced to knowledge that is beyond their books. Workshops organized during TECHWEEK introduce technologies to students and intent to stimulate their interest in cutting edge technologies of today. Elite companies from various streams of engineering have participated in TECHWEEK. TECHWEEK 2013 was all it promised to be - a true knowledge fair for all those who have in them a craving to learn. With workshops ranging from web-designing to robotics and embedded systems, from programming to 3-D animation, TECHWEEK 14 promises to be a true knowledge fair. Sessions on various upcoming technologies make TECHWEEK the perfect platform to expand one's knowledge base and enhance the technical skills.
