Robotics


Whether it’s mechanical, electrical engineering or computer science, when it comes to latest innovations, robotics is a field that holds the maximum potential. Which new technology and artificial intelligence programs trying to replicate human intelligence in machines; robotics forms the foundation of further advancements in the field of AI. This workshop in IEEE techweek covers all the basics in hardware and software required to make a robot, whether it be a mechanical bot or a Line Follower. For those interested in robotics, this workshop will have subject matter that you’ll remember when you are constructing your own personal bot.
