About IEEE-DTU


IEEE is the worlds largest professional association dedicated to advancing technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through IEEE's highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. With its itinerary lined up with events throughout the year , like TECHWEEK, REPERTOIRE followed by the annual tech fest TROIKA, IEEE DTU caters to the various technical and managerial needs of its members. The competitions held in these events are dynamic and diverse in nature, so as to cater to the needs of the people with varied interests and hence see participation from many colleges throughout the country. IEEE DTU is also well renowned for its publications - Echo - a magazine for freshers, IOTA - the annual technical magazine, TRIGGER - the TROIKA souvenir and The Troika Times. IEEE DTU takes great pride in imparting technical excellence as well as provides the perfect platform for the overall personality development of its members.
